The Scenic Route

A Relational Workshop for Couples Who Are Looking for More

led by

Nicole Crawford, LIMHP

Are you and your partner feeling stuck in the same roundabout of disconnection and disagreement?

Do you feel like your relationship is at a crossroads but you’re unsure of which way to go?

This workshop aims to help you and your partner:

  • Expand

    your understanding around communication that connects.

  • Explore

    how unmet needs become relationship roadblocks.

  • Engage

    in conflict with broadened perspectives that deepen intimacy.

Join other couples for an afternoon of education, conversation, and experiential learning. We will visit skills that foster reflection and deepen relationship understanding. We’ll find guidance in concepts from Emotionally Focused Therapy, Relational Life Therapy, Erotic Intelligence, and more!
